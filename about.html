About our Hotel

ATRIUM is the all new aparthotel in the prestigious “New Gudauri” complex The hotel offers a well-developed infrastructure, modern restaurant and bar, spa center and swimming pool.ATRIUM is a premium class hotel, with wooden decorated walls and soft floor corridors.

Concierge service is available for all guests 24/7. Top location of the building provides ski-in ski-out luxury – using ski depot ATRIUM guests have an instant access to the main ski slope and Gondola lift. Apartment 224 is located on the second floor. It provides a perfect view of the central square of New Gudauri complex as well as mountains.
